# FF3D (Fightfighter 3D Simulator)

**FF3D** is a comprehensive, squad-based tactical firefighting simulation game played from a first-person perspective, built with Unity. Players step into the boots of a frontline commander, responding to dynamic emergency calls, managing a squad of AI teammates, and utilizing a wide array of specialized equipment to save lives, control procedural fire spread, and execute complex rescue operations.

## Core Features

*   **Call Phase & Incident Generation:** Experience the tension of the dispatch desk. Receive calls, analyze transcripts, assess risks, and dynamically generate procedural incident scenarios (Random Seeds) based on your evaluations.
*   **Squad-Based AI:** Command a team of AI firefighters (Bots). Issue orders to extinguish fires, breach doors, rescue victims, manage hoses, or follow your lead. Bots have their own inventory system and interact dynamically with the environment.
*   **Advanced Fire & Smoke Simulation:** Engage with a highly detailed, procedural fire system. Fire spreads through clusters based on materials, leaves real-time scorch marks, and generates volumetric smoke hazards that affect visibility and oxygen levels.
*   **Extensive Tool Arsenal:** Access a robust Tool Wheel featuring thermal cameras, oxygen masks, various fire extinguishers, fire hoses, deployable ladders, rescue cushions, and victim stabilization kits.
*   **Dynamic Mission System:** Complex objectives, varying fail conditions (time limits, victim survival), and a comprehensive scoring snapshot system based on property damage, efficiency, and lives saved.
